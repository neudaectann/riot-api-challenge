Why DRAAAVO!?

Welcome to the League of DRAAAVO!

We all have that one friend, who relatively new to League of Legends or play from time to time.

It's fun to play with him, but he always ask "stupid" questions (of course from our point of view) like: "What item i should build next?" or "What would be my start items on "champion_name"?

While it can be fun to explain things for the first time, its become really strugle as the game progresses.

DRAAAVO was created to make you and "that one friend" happy and concentrated on actual gameplay, rather than thinking about items and builds.

Of course, DRAAAVO can substitute actual game knowledge or guides for respective champion,

but it surely can help newer players to step up their game.
